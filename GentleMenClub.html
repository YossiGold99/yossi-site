GentleMen Club

My goal in the project is to build a design language and a luxurious and refined whiskey brand. Whiskey Gentleman Club was created due to desire, understanding of the field and the lack of smoked and prestigious whiskey produced in Israel. My vision is to create an international Israeli whiskey brand and bring the world whiskey culture to Israel.
